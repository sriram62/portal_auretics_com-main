AcronymParticulars
MRP: Maximum Retail Price
The maximum retail price (MRP) is a manufacturer calculated price that is the highest price that can be charged (including all taxes, freight) for a product sold in the country.
DP: Distributor's Price
Price offered to each individual distributor for direct purchase from the company.
BV: Business Volume
Each product is assigned a BV, which is a percentage of DP and all commissions are calculated from BV.
PV: Point Value
PV are calculated as a ratio of BV. The current PV:BV ratio is 1:10*. PV is used for title progression. *PV:BV ratios are subject to change.
PBV: Personal Business Volume
PBV is the sum total of BV bought by a person personally in any particular month.
PPV: Personal Point Value
GBV: Group Business Volume
GBV is total BV from all the legs of a person (including BV from all director legs) and BV from sale done by him personally in any particular month, but excludes BV allocated to Super Plan.
GPV: Group Point Value
TBV: Team Business Volume
TBV is total BV from all the legs of a person (including BV from all director legs) and BV from sale done by him personally in any particular month, and includes BV allocated to Super Plan.
PGBV: Personal Group Business Volume
PGBV is total BV from all non-director legs of a person and BV from sale done by him personally in any particular month.
PGPV: Personal Group Point Value
Gross PBV:   Gross Personal Business Volume
Total BV achieved till date (cumulative).
Gross PPV:   Gross Personal Point Value
